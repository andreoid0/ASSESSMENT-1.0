Kare-Kare

Kare-Kare is a traditional Filipino oxtail stew with a rich peanut sauce, often served with bagoong (fermented shrimp paste) for extra flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg oxtail, cut into pieces
  • 1 banana heart, sliced
  • 1 bundle string beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup ground toasted rice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons annatto oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Bagoong (fermented shrimp paste) for serving

Method

  1. Boil oxtail in water until tender, then set aside.
  2. In a separate pan, saut√© garlic and onion in annatto oil.
  3. Add the boiled oxtail and some broth to the pan.
  4. Stir in peanut butter and ground toasted rice to thicken the sauce.
  5. Add banana heart, string beans, and eggplant, cooking until tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with rice and bagoong on the side. Enjoy!
